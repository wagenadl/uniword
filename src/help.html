Uniword 0.1.1 Keyword-based unicode character selector

(C) 2012 Daniel Wagenaar

To find a unicode character by its name or by any of its aliases, type words or parts of words into the text entry field at the top of the window. For instance, you could type “arr left” to get a list of left pointing arrows.
